Privacy Policy for Matrixon: Logic Fusion

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

Matrixon: Logic Fusion is a puzzle game designed to operate with minimal data collection. We do not personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number directly through the app.

2. Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device to enhance your gaming experience:

  • Level progress and completion status
  • Earned stars and game statistics
  • App settings (sound, vibration, theme preferences)

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our personal servers.

3. AdMob Advertising

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information automatically to provide personalized ads, including:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Google Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Crash and performance data

This information is used to provide relevant advertisements and to analyze the performance of the advertising network.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services in accordance with their privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. Their privacy policy can be found at https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Services: Used for platform-specific features on Android devices.

5. Your Rights

As a user of Matrixon, you have the right to:

  • Reset or delete all app data by clearing the app cache or uninstalling the app.
  • Opt-out of personalized ads through your Android device's "Ads" settings.
  • Review Google's data handling through the provided links.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for puzzle lovers of all ages. we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date on this page.
