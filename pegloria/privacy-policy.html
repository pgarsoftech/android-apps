Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 11, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Pegloria. Your privacy is paramount to us. This policy outlines how our application collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you enjoy our premium puzzle experience.

Information Collection

Pegloria is designed to be a privacy-first application. We do not require account registration or collect personal identifying information like names or email addresses. However, to provide a high-quality experience, we may collect:

  • Game Statistics: Locally stored data regarding moves, time, and high scores.
  • Device Identifiers: Anonymous IDs provided by mobile operating systems for ad attribution and basic analytics.
  • Technical Data: Device model, OS version, and language settings to ensure optimal rendering and performance.

Third-Party Services

To support the ongoing development of Pegloria, we utilize specialized third-party services that may collect data in accordance with their own policies:

  • Google AdMob: Used for displaying advertisements. They may collect location data (IP based) and advertising IDs to show relevant content.
  • Google Play Services: Used for game services and application integrity.

Children's Privacy

Pegloria complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. Our puzzle gameplay is family-friendly, and we ensure that advertisements shown prioritize safety and appropriateness.

GDPR Rights

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have specific data protection rights. You can reset your advertising identifier or delete your local game data at any time via your device settings or by clearing the application cache.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
